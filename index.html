There I Stand

Within the restlessness of humanity

A tapestry of noise

A compilation of complexity

There I stand in poise

I close my eyes and fade away

To somewhere very new

Or perhaps a place of fantasies

To that same special hue

The wind whistles upon entry

Making forest trees speak

There I stand silent

With snow tears down my cheek

I feel a poking on my shoulder though

So it's time to say goodbye

To that special place of silence

Which is withered through time
